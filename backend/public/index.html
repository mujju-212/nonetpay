Why NONETPAY

Built for the Real World

Where networks fail, NONETPAY keeps transactions flowing.

100% Offline Payments

Generate and receive signed vouchers with zero internet. Sync later when connectivity returns.

Cryptographic Security

Every voucher signed with ECDSA secp256k1 — same cryptography as Bitcoin. Impossible to forge.

QR Code Payments

Scan merchant QR to pay instantly. Works even with airplane mode on both devices.

Auto Background Sync

Transactions queue locally and sync automatically the moment internet is restored.

No Double Spend

Each voucher has a unique ID. Server rejects duplicates — even if presented multiple times offline.

AI Spending Insights

Built-in AI analyzes spending patterns and gives smart financial suggestions in natural language.

The Flow

How It Works

Four steps. Entirely offline.

1

Add Balance Online

Top up wallet via Razorpay (UPI, cards). Balance cached locally.

2

Pay Offline via QR

Scan merchant QR → signed voucher generated → balance deducted locally. No internet needed.

3

Merchant Accepts

Merchant scans voucher QR and stores it. When either reconnects, it syncs and merchant is credited.

Server Verifies & Settles

Backend verifies ECDSA signature, rejects duplicates, and settles — guaranteeing end-to-end integrity.
